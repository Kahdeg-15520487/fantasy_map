# Nightstar Mansion 

*The mansion is situated deep in the dark forest, in uncharted lands. Recently a party of orcs rediscovered it, making it their headquarters. Rumors say that the mansion is rich with ancient books.*

1. A battered crate holds an uncanny, made of an unknown material object.
2. A notable gate with two keyholes to the west.
3. An incenerated body of a gnome, a key nearby.
4. A strange, glowing pin in a curio case.
5. A chest containing a key.
