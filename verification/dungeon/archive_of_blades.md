# Archive of Blades 

*For years the archive remained sealed. Lately a band of bandits rediscovered it, making it their center of operation. The archive is a place of growth of a highly valued specie of blooms.*

1. A lavishly decorated double door with two keyholes on the eastern wall of the small chamber.
2. A broken strongbox containing a key.
3. A pile of rubble hides some gold.
4. A stuffed halfling barbarian with a mysterious, bleeding object in their hands.
5. The small room is filled with dense mist. It turns into ashes when breathed in.
6. A rear entrance into the archive.
7. A box holds a key.
8. A corpse of a bandit, a scarf, an enchanted flail and a potion of speed close to it.
