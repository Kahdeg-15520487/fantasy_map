# Catacombs of Rhogalasketh 

*For years the catacombs of Rhogalasketh were considered lost. Recently a pack of manticores have made its lair here. It is rumored that Karhogar, a legendary spellbook, is hidden here.*

1. A handsome elf. Wants to join you.
2. Some gold among rubble on the ground.
3. A bottomless pit, teleports a person outside the catacombs if a coin is dropped into it.
4. A corpse of a dwarf, a doll of health close to it.
5. A mouth-shaped iron gate to the south.
6. A tapestry on the wall depicting a scene of ascension.
7. An intricate puzzle, turns a person to stone when solved.
